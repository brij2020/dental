1. Consent to Treatment

I, the undersigned patient (or legal guardian), hereby acknowledge that I have been fully informed by my dentist of the nature, purpose, benefits, risks, alternatives, and possible complications of the recommended dental treatment procedures. These procedures may include, but are not limited to:

  • Dental examinations and diagnostic radiographs (X-rays)
  • Dental cleanings and periodontal treatments
  • Restorative procedures (fillings, crowns, bridges)
  • Endodontic therapy (root canal treatment)
  • Tooth extractions and oral surgery
  • Any other procedures deemed necessary during the course of treatment

2. Understanding of Treatment Factors

I acknowledge that I understand the following:

Benefits: The intended benefits of dental treatment include improvement of oral health, relief of pain or discomfort, prevention of disease progression, and restoration of function and/or aesthetics.

Risks and Complications: Potential risks and complications may include, but are not limited to, infection, bleeding, swelling, bruising, allergic reactions to medications or anesthetics, nerve injury, tooth or restoration fracture, changes in bite, temporary or permanent numbness, and the possible failure of restorations requiring additional treatment.

Lifestyle and Compliance Factors: I understand that smoking, inadequate oral hygiene, or failure to follow post-operative instructions may increase the likelihood of complications and negatively affect treatment outcomes.

Alternatives: I have been informed of alternative treatment options, including the option of no treatment. I understand that declining treatment may result in worsening conditions, including pain, infection, tooth loss, or other serious oral health problems.

3. Changes in Treatment Plan

I understand that unforeseen conditions may arise during treatment, such as hidden decay or structural damage, which may require modification of the original treatment plan. I authorize my dentist to perform any additional procedures deemed clinically necessary under such circumstances.

4. Authorization and Acknowledgment

I confirm that I have had the opportunity to ask questions regarding my treatment, and all questions have been answered to my satisfaction. I voluntarily consent to the recommended dental treatment and authorize my dentist and designated staff to perform necessary procedures, including the use of local anesthesia, medications, and diagnostic radiographs as required.
